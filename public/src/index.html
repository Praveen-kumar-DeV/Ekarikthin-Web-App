About Ekarithin 2023

Ekarikthin is an annual techno-cultural festival hosted by National Institute of Technology Nagaland, in north-east India, Lasting three days, Ekarikthin is the largest techno-cultural festival in Nagalandand provides a platform for talented youths in various cultural and technical events. Students from all over Nagaland participate.

It provides a platform to young nurturing,talented youths to showcase their talents in various events related to cultural , technical and sports.

EXCITING EVENTS FOR EVERYONE

The events and competitions at Ekarikthin belong to a variety of cultural categories such as Music, Dance, Literary, Quiz, Drama, Fine Arts, Film, and many more. Here participants get to showcase their talents and win laurels for their respective colleges.
