Location

The National Institute of Technology (NIT) Nagaland is located in the city of Dimapur, the commercial hub of Nagaland. Dimapur is the largest city in the state, situated in the western part of Nagaland and is connected to the rest of the country by rail, road, and air. The NIT Nagaland campus is located approximately 9 kilometers from Dimapur Railway Station and 7 kilometers from Dimapur Airport.
